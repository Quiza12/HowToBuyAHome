Introduction

Hi, I'm Matt, a born and bred Sydneysider who is trying to realise his (and everyone else's dream) of buying a property.

When I started looking in mid-2019, I found that a lot of the information related to property buying I was relying on was either anecdotal or a mix of disparate blog posts I'd found on the Internet.

This website is my attempt at synthesizing my learnings and experience into the most objective guide possible, complete with a few free Excel/Numbers templates that I used to help track my property findings and get a rough estimate of what my mortgage repayments might be.

Full disclosure, my father is an independent mortgage broker, whom I plug here and below. He has helped quite a few first home buyers achieve their dreams, so if you want an experienced, trusted, no-nonsense broker, feel free to reach out to him on his website.
